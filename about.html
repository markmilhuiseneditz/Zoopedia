Who We Are

Zoopedia is an online platform designed to provide information about zoos, animals, and wildlife parks around the world. We aim to make learning about animals fun and accessible for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate people about wildlife conservation and help users discover different animal species and zoos easily.

Our Vision

We envision becoming a leading platform for wildlife education, inspiring people to care for animals and protect nature.
